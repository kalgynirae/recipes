---
title: Calico Beans
---

This recipe is from Ann Titus.

Brown:

*   1 lb ground beef
*   1/2 lb bacon, cut up

Add:

*   1 cup diced onions

Drain off fat.

Add:

*   1 can kidney beans, drained
*   1 can pork and beans, drained
*   1 can butter beans, drained
*   1/4 cup brown sugar
*   1/4 cup white sugar
*   1 tablespoon vinegar
*   1 teaspoon dry mustard
*   1/2 cup ketchup

Bake at 350Â°F for 40 minutes.
