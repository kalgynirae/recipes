% Chicken Curry (DRAFT)
% Colin Chan
% 2015-09-12

* 1/4 cup vegetable oil
* 2 cinnamon sticks
* 6 green cardamom pods
* 8 cloves
* 1 tsp cumin seeds
* 4 large onions, diced finely
* 1(?) tsp salt

Cook for a long time, until onions are very soft.

* 2 tbsp minced garlic
* 2 tsp curry powder
* 1 cup chicken broth
* 10 chicken thighs

Simmer until done.
